Welcome to ChemoPar-db

ChemoPar-db contains all available chemokine PDB structures which are organized, annotated, and aligned. You can browse the UniProt reviewed chemokine protein entries or you can search the database for all structures.

Chemokine-Partner interaction analysis

Dive into our analysis of chemokine-partner complexes, where we unlock the secrets of their interactions through detailed structural examination. By annotating binding regions on chemokines, our research illuminates the different binding regions on chemokines. This concise exploration not only advances our understanding of chemokine mechanisms but also opens new avenues for therapeutic innovation.

About

Chemokines are a family of small signaling proteins (chemotactic cytokines) that play crucial roles in cellular signaling processes, particularly in the immune system. They are involved in guiding and regulating the movement of immune cells within the body, helping to orchestrate the immune response to various pathogens and inflammatory signals.

Read more about the general structural features of chemokines and how this database was constructed on the about page.
